A LITTLE BIT ABOUT US

We started Coffins Coffee Company as a family with a passion for coffee and a mission to inject some flavor into an otherwise boring landscape of coffee companies. We knew that we could bring a new and exciting approach to coffee to the masses. We started with flavors like Strawberry Cheesecake and Maple Bacon and pretty quickly discovered that so many people were craving what we were trying to build, even if they didn’t realize it. Now, still family-owned and operated with over 50 amazing employees from our local communities, we’re shipping our products all over the world. It’s been a hell of a journey so far and we can’t wait to see where we can go from here!
